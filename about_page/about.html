our story

  We are enviromentally focused local business proud to service the community. Our cafe not only sells premium organic coffees and teas, but also locally made desserts such as Macarons, Cakes, and Waffles. We are proud to say that all of our coffees and teas are organic, and sourced from other local businesses, or though fair trade.
  Our drive to bring the best quality resources means that our ingredients come from local businesses such as Republica Roasters, and other small local businesses including areas such as Fort Langley. We are constantly seeking to make environmentally friendly business decisions, and we hope to serve our community for many years. As such, we have taken measures to reduce plastic waste and move to reusable utensils or wooden alternatives.
 If you have suggestions for our improvements, or have any concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to us on ur Contact Us page.
 We look forward to seeing you soon!
